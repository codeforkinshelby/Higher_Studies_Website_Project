Crafting the Perfect Application













Academics


Cracking the higher studies dream offer is sure difficult, but not if you have the right companion by your side. Unlike indian colleges, abroad universities select candidates on a more hoslistic manner by closely reviewing their applications. Thus, it is essential you craft the perfect application to stay in the foray of

getting your dream admit!



Elements of an ideal candidate:


  1. Good X & XII board exam scores (>90%)
  2. CGPA - 8.0+
  3. GRE score: 320+ (Quants - 165+)(Verbal - 155+)
  4. IELTS - 7.0+ (or) TOEFL - 90+/120
  5. Research paper in distinguished journal or conference is desirable
  6. Well rounded person i.e. has interest beyond academics
  7. Active participant in NGO activities
  8. Internships related to desired field
